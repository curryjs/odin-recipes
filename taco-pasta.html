Taco Pasta

Description

A new and unique take on pasta, this version is basically a taco bowl but in pasta form

Ingredients

  • One pound ground beef
  • One chopped onion
  • One bellpepper
  • One tablespoon Tomato Paste
  • One tablespoon flour
  • One tablespoon chili powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Paprika
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Water
  • Milk
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Rotini Pasta
  • Black Beans
  • Cheddar Cheese
  • Avocado

Directions

  1. Cook beef, onion, and peppers in large dutch oven
  2. Add pasta, flour, seasonings and stir to coat
  3. Add water, milk, tomatoes and bring to boil
  4. Add pasta and reduce heat to cook to al dente
  5. Add in black beans and cheese, stir until melted
  6. Top with avocado and serve
