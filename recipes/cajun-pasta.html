Cajun Pasta

Description

This delicious pasta is easy to make with one pot and finishes in just 30 minutes. Using cajun andouille along with rotini pasta and a few other simple ingredients, never go hungry again!

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces Andouille Sausage
  • Olive Oil
  • 1/4 cup diced onion
  • 1 diced bellpepper
  • 1 jalapeno
  • Minced Garlic
  • 10 ounces crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cups Chicken Stock
  • 1 tablespoon cajun seasoning
  • 10 ounces rotini pasta
  • 4 ounces cream cheese

Steps

  1. Chop up sausage, onion, and pepper and add to pot on medium high heat for 5 minutes
  2. Dice garlic and add to pot for 30 seconds
  3. Add seasoning, tomatoes, and chicken stock and bring to boil
  4. Add in pasta, reduce heat and simmer covered for 10 minutes
  5. Add in cream cheese, stir, and continue to simmer for 5 more minutes
