Bourbon chicken

Description

A delicious and savory rice and chicken recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless chicken breast
  • 1 Garlic Clove
  • 1/4 teaspoon ginger
  • 3/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 1/4 cup apple juice
  • 1/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons ketchup
  • 1 tbs cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 1/3 cup soy sauce

Directions

  1. Heat oil in large skillet and add diced chicken breast
  2. Cook until lightly browned
  3. Remove chicekn and add remaining ingredients
  4. Heat over medium heat until well dissolved
  5. Return chicken and bring to boil
  6. Reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes
